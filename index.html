About

I was born in Lahore, Pakistan, and now live in New York, where I’m pursuing my passion for Communication Design at City Tech. I’m drawn to creative storytelling that blends art, design, and culture. Whether I’m working on a digital illustration or capturing an emotional photograph, I always aim to create something that feels personal, warm, and visually meaningful.

Bio

I’m a multidisciplinary designer and photographer who enjoys exploring design in many forms — from digital illustration and graphic layouts to branding and color systems. My creative process often starts with emotion and atmosphere, and I love bringing human stories to life through my work. Alongside design, I also enjoy web design as a side passion, where I experiment with HTML and CSS to showcase my work online.
